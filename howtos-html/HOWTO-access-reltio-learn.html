HOWTO: Access Reltio Learn

Reltio Learn is Reltio's training and certification platform — use it to complete role-based learning paths, register for live events, and earn industry-recognized credentials.

Overview

This guide walks you through registering for a Reltio Learn account if you don't have one, signing in if you do, and enrolling in certifications. Reltio Learn uses separate credentials from the Reltio Context Intelligence Platform and is reserved for current Reltio customers and partners.

This guide is for these Reltio roles: Business User, Data Product Owner, Data Steward, Developer, Reltio Configurator, Solution Architect, System Administrator. For more information on data unification roles in the Reltio Context Intelligence Platform, see About roles.

1. Getting started

Reltio Learn is at learn.reltio.com and is separate from your Reltio tenant login. You need a Reltio Learn account to access any training content, live events, or certifications.

Before you begin, confirm the following:

  • Business email address — you must use a valid Reltio customer or partner business email. Personal email addresses are not accepted.
  • Access type — Reltio Learn is reserved for current Reltio customers and partners. If you're unsure whether your organization qualifies, contact your Reltio account team.
  • Existing account — if you previously used Reltio Academy, your account may already exist in Reltio Learn. Try signing in before registering.

Learn more: Get started with Reltio Learn

2. Register for a Reltio Learn account

If you don't have a Reltio Learn account, follow these steps to request access. The Reltio training team reviews registrations manually — allow up to 48 hours for approval.

  1. Open Reltio Learn in your browser.

  2. On the Sign In page, select New User? Register.

  3. Complete the registration form using your business email address.

  4. In the Additional fields section, select the role that best describes your work:

    • Data Steward
    • System Administrator
    • Technical Delivery Manager
    • Technical Consultant
    • Solution Architect

    Note: Reltio Learn roles may combine multiple product personas into a single training role. For example, the Technical Consultant role covers responsibilities described under the Reltio Configurator and Developer personas. For more information, see About roles.

  5. Submit the registration request.

After your access is approved, you'll receive a confirmation. Then follow the steps in Sign in to Reltio Learn.

Learn more: Register for a Reltio Learn account

3. Sign in to Reltio Learn

If you already have an approved Reltio Learn account, sign in as follows:

  1. Open Reltio Learn in your browser.

  2. On the Sign In page, enter your business email address as your username.

  3. Enter your password.

    Important: If your Reltio Learn account was created before January 15, 2026, sign in once using the temporary password Welcome10. You'll be prompted to create a new password immediately after.

  4. Complete the sign-in process.

After you sign in, the Reltio Learn home page opens. From there you can resume in-progress courses, access your assigned learning path, browse the Course Catalog, register for Live Events, and explore certification tracks organized by core Reltio components such as APIs, Match & Merge, and Data Loader.

Tip: Visit the What's New & Release Resources page to stay current with new training content.

Learn more: Sign in to Reltio Learn

4. Enroll in certifications

Reltio certifications provide industry-recognized credentials and are taken with advanced proctoring for secure, high-integrity assessments. You must have a Reltio Learn account to enroll.

  1. Sign in to Reltio Learn.
  2. On the Welcome page, scroll down to Certifications.
  3. Select the certification you want and enroll.
  4. If it's your first attempt, complete any noted prerequisites and read the Reltio Certification Exam Guide in the Syllabus of your selected certification.
  5. Complete the exam.
  6. Results are sent via email.
  7. If you pass, you'll receive an email from Credly with instructions to claim your digital credential.
  8. Log in to Credly via the link in the email:
    • If you don't have a Credly account, create one.
    • If you already have one, go to your profile page to view your awarded credential.
  9. Send feedback to training@reltio.com.

After earning your certification, continue exploring additional learning paths and resources in Reltio Learn.

Learn more: Get started with Reltio Certifications

6. Glossary

Certifications: industry-recognized credentials issued by Reltio upon passing a proctored exam. Credentials are distributed as digital badges through Credly.

Credly: the third-party digital credential platform Reltio uses to issue and share certification badges.

Learning path: a curated sequence of courses in Reltio Learn organized by role, helping you build skills in a structured order.

Reltio Learn: Reltio's training and certification platform at learn.reltio.com, offering role-based learning paths, live events, and certification programs for Reltio customers and partners.
