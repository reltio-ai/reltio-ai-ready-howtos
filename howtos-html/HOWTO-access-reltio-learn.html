1. What is Reltio Learn?

Reltio Learn is Reltio's training platform for developing your skills and expertise in data unification and Master Data Management (MDM). It offers:

  • Role-based learning paths that build your skills whether you're preparing for an implementation, becoming a Data Steward, or upskilling on best practices
  • Instructor-led sessions (Live Events)
  • Certifications with industry-recognized credentials via Credly

Reltio Learn uses separate credentials from the Reltio Context Intelligence Platform — your Reltio platform login won't work here.

Note: If you're an existing Reltio Academy user, you still access training through Reltio Learn — the platform has been consolidated.

2. Register for a Reltio Learn account

If you don't yet have an account, follow these steps to request access.

Steps

  1. Open Reltio Learn in your browser.
  2. On the Sign In page, select New User? Register.
  3. Complete the registration form using your business email address.
  4. In the Additional fields section, select your role:
    • Data Steward
    • System Administrator
    • Technical Delivery Manager
    • Technical Consultant
    • Solution Architect

Note: Reltio Learn roles may combine multiple product personas into a single training role. For example, the Technical Consultant role includes responsibilities described under the Reltio Configurator and Developer personas. For more information, see About roles.

  1. Submit the registration request.

What happens next

The Reltio training team reviews your registration and enables access after approval. Allow up to 48 hours for the review.

3. Sign in to Reltio Learn

Once you have an approved account, sign in to start learning.

Steps

  1. Open Reltio Learn in your browser.
  2. On the Sign In page, enter your business email address as your username.
  3. Enter your password.

Important: If your account was migrated from Reltio Academy (created before January 15, 2026), your temporary password is Welcome10. You'll be prompted to change it on first login.

  1. Complete the sign-in process.

What you'll see

After you sign in, the Reltio Learn home page opens. You can:

  • View your in-progress courses
  • Access your assigned learning path
  • Resume where you left off

If it's your first time signing in, the home page displays options to:

  • Browse the Reltio Learn Course Catalog
  • Register for Live Events
  • Explore Reltio Certification paths

4. Explore courses and learning paths

Reltio Learn organizes content around your role and the Reltio components you work with. After signing in, you can access certification tracks and learning plans organized by core Reltio components such as:

  • APIs
  • Match & Merge
  • Data Loader

To stay current with new training content, visit the What's New & Release Resources page.

Tip: Check the What's New page regularly — Reltio adds new learning paths and content with each release.

5. Get certified

Reltio Certifications provide industry-recognized credentials to validate your expertise. Exams use advanced proctoring for secure, high-integrity assessments.

Prerequisites

You need a Reltio Learn account before you can enroll in certifications.

Steps

  1. Log in to Reltio Learn.
  2. On the Welcome page, scroll down to Certifications.
  3. Select and enroll in the certification you want.
  4. First time? Complete any noted prerequisites and the Reltio Certification Exam Guide in the Syllabus of the selected certification.
  5. Complete the exam.
  6. Results are sent via email.
  7. If you pass, you'll receive an email from Credly with instructions to claim your credential.
  8. Log in to Credly via the link in your email.
    • If you don't have a Credly account, create one.
    • If you already have an account, go to your profile page to view your awarded credential.
  9. Learn how to share your credential here.
  10. Share feedback to training@reltio.com.

After earning your certification, explore additional learning paths and resources in Reltio Learn.

6. Access the Partner Portal

If you're a Reltio implementation partner, the Reltio Partner Portal provides resources to help you succeed. If you don't already have an account, request one from the portal.

Note: The Partner Portal is separate from Reltio Learn. It's designed specifically for implementation partners.

7. Get help from Reltio Support

If you run into issues with Reltio Learn or need help with the Reltio platform, the Reltio Support Portal offers Knowledge Base articles, a community forum, and an AI assistant called Sage.
